About B Fresh

BFresh Catering provides catering and event management services and is well known in Ranchi, Jharkhand area for its creative cuisine, capitol style and competitive pricing. We can assist you with every aspect of your event to ensure it is a smashing success!!! We are new in market, fresh like new leaves, with bright innovative ideas, hoping to revolutionize the catering field and set an example amongst rivals.

We take care of all the details, from preparation to clean up. Our prices are disclosed up front in your event contract, and we do not impose hidden charges. Our team of highly qualified professionals will make any event a pleasure. From weddings to cocktail parties, or anything in between, BFresh Catering is your best choice.

Our food reflects our commitment to exceptional quality. All meals are prepared under the guidance of one of the best chefs. Our menu items are prepared with a passion for perfection, and your guests will be raving about the wonderful food for days to come. New dishes are being added regularly to our menu which has been developed over the past two decades. We serve traditional, elegant fare, with a contemporary flair!
